Who is this for?

This platform is designed for golf enthusiasts—mainly males in their 20s–50s—who want to buy, sell, or discover golf club memberships. Whether you’re an individual seller, a buyer, or a golf course looking to recruit new members, GolfMarket is your trusted marketplace.

  • Individuals selling unneeded memberships
  • Buyers seeking new memberships
  • Golf courses officially listing and recruiting new members
  • Anyone looking for information on joining a specific golf club

Membership Types & Price Ranges

We specialize in regular golf club memberships (individual name memberships) from courses all across Japan.
Typical price range: ¥500,000 – ¥3,000,000
Premium memberships: ¥5,000,000 – ¥10,000,000+
